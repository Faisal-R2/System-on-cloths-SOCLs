SoCl: Embroidered Electronic System on Cloth

Designing embroidered electronic components and circuits directly on fabric.

SoCl transforms electrical specifications and conductive thread properties into manufacturable textile circuit geometries, stitch-level toolpaths, and machine-ready embroidery files.

Embroidered Component Library

SoCl introduces fabric-based components including embroidered resistors, capacitors, inductors, conductive traces, contact pads, electrodes, and transistor/IC interfaces.

Material-Aware Design

Conductive thread properties, stitch spacing, and textile substrate behavior are used to generate manufacturable circuit geometries from target electrical specifications.

Automated Toolpath Generation

SoCl converts component geometries into stitch-level embroidery toolpaths, managing conductive paths, jumps, trims, and electrical isolation for machine-ready embroidery files.

Smart Textile Applications

The component library supports ECG, EMG, force sensing, sweat sensing, RFID antennas, touch sensors, temperature sensors, and electrical stimulation electrodes.
